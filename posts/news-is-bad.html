News Is Bad

09-11-2021

I remember at some point in 2019 i was living as a news junkie. I was like one of those person who had 4 different news apps in phone and a dozen news websites bookmarked in browser. I, like other news junkies thought it is necessary to know whatever is happening in society. I enjoyed learning what funny thing did Trump said in interview earlier today and also the creative excuses Greta is making to bunk her classes.

Secondly, News contains lot of bugs in it. As news is of dynamic in nature, news channels compete with each other to be the first to telecast a news. Obviously when anything happens, in the initial stages it provides only limited information. Because of this we can see that when people make opinions based on news they are mostly proved wrong in long run. for example, when Corona virus first came, news channels based on early/limited info told that it will wipe out humanity, but here after almost two years we can see that it was an over-exaggerated. Read history. Yes you are not up to date when you read history books instead of watching news but you are more closer to truth than the average news junkie. When experts spend more time analyzing an event we get more accurate picture of it. For example, now we know more about Soviet Union than the people who lived at the time of Soviet Union did. Plus history is far less volatile than news. New findings time to time corrects the history of a particular events but still history is far-far more stable than news.

finally, we can say that news is like a rolling release Linux distribution, you get lot of bugs with it, things get fast changes, and you always live in fear than an update (news) might break your system.(brain)

History on the other hand is like stable long-term support Linux distribution. It is tested well before it is released. and you will always get small bug fixes every now and then. History like debian can be boring to some people but you know what, you can trust it.
